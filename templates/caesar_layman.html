Caesar Cipher - Layman's Explanation

Imagine you have a secret message, and you want to hide it by shifting each letter a little bit. Let's say your message is 'HELLO'. If we shift every letter forward by 3 places in the alphabet, 'H' becomes 'K', 'E' becomes 'H', and so on. So, 'HELLO' would become 'KHOOR'. It's like having a secret code where only people who know the right number (called the 'shift') can read the message.
